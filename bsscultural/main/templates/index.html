BSS Cultural Mission

BSS Cultural Mission is a division of Bharat Sevak Samaj to provide total solutions in the arena of Arts and Culture and to promote high end Arts and Cultural education and training all over India. Bharat Sevak Samaj Cultural Mission

We admit students irrespective of their age and help them to gain certification of Bharat Sevak Samaj. One can pursue music along with school studies. We follow our own syllabus which offers basic to advance level Music study. Classes are organised in a highly conducive learning environment that helps students of any age to shed all their inhibitions and encourage them to hone their skills in music. Our syllabus opens up various aspects of the music from the very beginning of a students study and helps him to build a professionally viable career. Another advantage of the syllabus is that it helps to reap the benefits of Gurukulavidyabhysa from a class room level teaching.
